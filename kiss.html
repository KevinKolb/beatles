How this KISS chart is scored

Each song uses the same four-lane model as the Beatles page: writing 45, lead vocal 20, backing vocal 10, and playing 25. Song titles, writers, lead vocalists, releases, and years are seeded from the public List of songs recorded by Kiss table.

Listed writers and shared lead vocals split evenly. Backing vocals are inferred from the credited lead singer(s) and the album-era playing lineup, while non-original members, outside writers, and session players are grouped as Other. Playing points use album-era defaults rather than exhaustive session-by-session credits.
